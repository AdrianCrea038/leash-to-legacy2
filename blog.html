Positive Reinforcement: Why It Works for Your Dog

At Leash to Legacy we believe that training should be a joyful experience for both you and your pup. Positive reinforcement isn't just a method — it's a philosophy that builds trust, keeps your dog attentive, and creates a stronger bond.

We start with basics like sit, down, and touch/boop in the comfort of your home. Seeing your house and your dog in action helps us design a teaching schedule the whole family can be part of. Patience and repetition are key, and we're here to support you every step of the way.

Reach out on our contact page — you're going to make a great team!
